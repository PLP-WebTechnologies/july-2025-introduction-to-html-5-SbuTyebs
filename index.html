What is PLP?

Power Learn Project (PLP) is a transformative initiative aimed at empowering individuals with the skills needed for the digital economy. It focuses on providing quality education and practical experience in web development and other tech fields.

What Specializations are Offered?

  • Web Development
  • Data Science
  • Software Engineering

Web Development

The Web Development specialization equips learners with the skills to build dynamic and responsive websites. It covers HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and backend technologies.

Data Science

The Data Science specialization focuses on data analysis, machine learning, and statistical modeling. It prepares learners to extract insights from data and make data-driven decisions.

Software Engineering

The Software Engineering specialization provides a comprehensive understanding of software development processes, including design, testing, and deployment. It prepares learners for roles in software development teams.

Becoming a PLP Graduate

To graduate from PLP, learners must complete the required coursework, participate in projects, and demonstrate proficiency in their chosen specialization. This includes hands-on experience and collaboration with peers.

Being a PLP Alumni

As a PLP alumni, you will have access to a network of professionals, job placement assistance, and ongoing learning opportunities. You will be well-equipped to enter the tech industry and make a meaningful impact.
